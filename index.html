Readership Map

Options

Simulated:

  • Draws data from data.json, which contains Google Analytics data for the Scholars Archive account from October 14, 2015.
  • The article title, author, etc., shown in the info windows for each marker are not the actual articles viewed/downloaded, but were matched randomly to the Analytics data from a set of actual ETDs.
  • The simulation always starts at 11:00 AM (a relatively high-traffic time).

Live:

  • Draws data from a Google Analytics account of the user who authorizes access by signing into a Google account.
  • No article information will be shown.
  • The data are real time (with a delay of about 20 minutes).
